# Progression and endgame

## Three tiers of goals

| Tier | Name | What it means | When |
|------|------|---------------|------|
| 1 | **Season path** | Four themed objectives per calendar season; claim modest resource rewards within the cycle | Each of the 4 weeks |
| 2 | **Cycle legend** | Chase the 13 achievements/trophies; cycle score = trophy points earned that cycle | Weeks 2–4 |
| 3 | **Skyline** | Upgrade any building to level 10 — optional prestige, not required to “finish” a cycle | Week 4 stretch |

There is **no single win state**. Competitive players aim for leaderboard rank before the wipe; builders chase trophies; Skyline is a bragging-rights capstone.

## Pacing (active daily player)

| Phase | Week | Typical state | Reachable goals |
|-------|------|---------------|-----------------|
| Opening | 1 (Spring) | TH2–3, first military | Season objectives, early achievements |
| Mid | 2–3 | TH4–5, raids, navy/spies | Score 150+, Master Builder, Thriving Realm |
| Late | 4 (Winter) | TH5–7, some L8–9 | Remaining trophies, Skyline if focused |

**Perfect kingdom** (all 17 types at level 10) is **not realistic** in one 28-day cycle — exponential costs and upgrade times cap how far you can push.

## Full wipe every cycle

At each **4-week cycle boundary**, the kingdom is **completely reset**:

| Wiped | Preserved |
|-------|-----------|
| All buildings (TH → 1 only) | Player account |
| Army, missions, spy ops, raids | Peaceful mode (if opted in) |
| Resources → 200G / 200F / 150W / 100S | Trophy history + lifetime trophy points |
| Population → 10 | — |

- **No cross-cycle carryover** — no bonus starters, retained buildings, or legacy buffs (may revisit later).
- **Season objective rewards** are within-cycle only; unclaimed objectives are lost on wipe.
- Manual **Kingdom reset** (Settings) uses the same starter pack without waiting for the cycle boundary.

## Cycle finale (player experience)

When a cycle ends:

1. Server performs a full wipe to the fixed starter pack.
2. A **cycle recap** shows trophies earned in the ending cycle, trophy points, and leaderboard rank (if not peaceful).
3. Player starts fresh — trophies remain on their permanent record.

## Week 4 production boost

The **fourth week** of each cycle (Winter) grants **+5%** production on top of season and realm-event modifiers. This softens the winter penalty during the cycle finale without removing seasonal identity.

## Future (not implemented in v1)

- Leaderboard prizes at cycle end.
- Additional achievement types for season-specific feats.
